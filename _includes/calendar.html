Fix-it Café: Textile Edition & Open Houses

Saturday, November 23 from 2:00 to 4:00 pm

Are your beloved jeans getting frayed at the knees? Have you got buttons popping off your favourite jacket, or holes in your favourite sweater? Or just picked up that learn-to-knit project from last year and can't figure out where you left off in your pattern?

Meta Makers is here to help! Bring your textiles in need of some love down to Meta Makers' Fix-it Café: Textile Edition and we'll do our best to help you fix those holes, reinforce those knees and elbows, or get you back on track with your sewing, knitting, and crochet projects. Bring your sewing materials if you have them, or borrow some of ours.
