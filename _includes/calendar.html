CNC Training Course

Starting February 23rd

Meta Makers is offering a CNC and Mastercam training course. The course teaches introductory 2D programming using Mastercam. The programmed project will then be cut by the student on our CNC router.

The next opening will be starting February 23rd and running for 4 weeks. The classes start at 10:00 am and last 5 hours.

Cost for members is $150.00. Non members cost is $450.00, which includes a 3-month membership, and 10 free hours assisted machine time. Some conditions apply.

To enroll in the course email info@metamakers.org or talk to one of our directors.
