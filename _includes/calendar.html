Grand Opening!

You're invited to our Grand Opening: Saturday, May 25th 2-4pm

628 Monmouth Road

Free

You're invited to our Grand Opening on Saturday, May 25th from 2-4pm! Come and see what Windsor-Essex's only community makerspace has to offer.

A makerspace is like a shared community workshop where you can access tools and resources for making things. At our grand opening, you'll see our members demo the creative activities they enjoy doing at the makerspace: 3D printing, Fibre Arts, Letterpress printing, CNC, Woodworking, Stained Glass and more! What you would make at Meta Makers?

This event is also an important milestone for us: we opened our doors in 2019, survived the pandemic, and now we're celebrating a grand opening in a larger space with new equipment funded by the Government of Canada under the Community Services Recovery Fund (CSRF). You'll see our new CNC machine, laser engraver, 3D printers, and more. We want all of Windsor-Essex to see that these resources are available to them at Meta Makers!
